68 (1949). Resolution of 10 February 1949 
[8/1252] 

The Security Council

Resolves that General Assembly resolution 192 (III) of 19 November 1948, as contained in document 8/ 1216, (17) be transmitted to the Commission for Conventional Armaments for action according to its terms.

Footnotes

(17) See Official Records of the Security Council, Fourth Year. Supplement for January 1949.
