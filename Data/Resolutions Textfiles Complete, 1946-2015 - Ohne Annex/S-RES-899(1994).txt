The Security Council,  
Recalling its resolution 833 (1993) of 27 May 1993,  
Having considered the Secretary-General's letter of 22 February 1994 (document S/1994/240) concerning the matter of the Iraqi private citizens and their assets which remained on Kuwaiti territory following the demarcation of the international boundary between Iraq and Kuwait, and welcoming the developments and arrangements described therein,  
Acting under Chapter VII of the Charter of the United Nations,  
Decides that the compensation payments to be made pursuant to the arrangements described in the Secretary-General's letter of 22 February 1994 may be remitted to the private citizens concerned in Iraq, notwithstanding the provisions of resolution 661 (1990) .
