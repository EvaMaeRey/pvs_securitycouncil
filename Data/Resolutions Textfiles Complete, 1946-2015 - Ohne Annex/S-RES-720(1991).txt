The Security Council,  
Having considered the question of the recommendation for the appointment of the Secretary-General of the United Nations,  
Recommends to the General Assembly that Boutros Boutros Ghali be appointed Secretary-General of the United Nations for a term of office from 1 January 1992 to 31 December 1996.
