The Security Council,  
Recalling its resolution 719 (1991) of 6 November 1991,  
Recalling also its resolution 729 (1992) of 14 January 1992,  
1.       Approves the report of the Secretary-General of 14 January 1992 (S/23421);  
2.       Decides, in accordance with the recommendation in paragraph 7 of the report, to terminate the mandate of the United Nations Observer Group in Central America (ONUCA) with effect from 17 January 1992.
