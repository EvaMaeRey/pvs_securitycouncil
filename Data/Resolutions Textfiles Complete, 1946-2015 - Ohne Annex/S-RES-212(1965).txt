Resolution 212 (1965) of 20 September 1965 

The Security Council, 

Having examined the application of the Maldive Islands for admission to the United Nations, (41)

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Maldive Islands be admitted to membership in the United Nations.


Footnotes

(41) Ibid., Supplement for July, August and September 1965, document S/6645.
