Resolution 376 (1975) of 17 October 1975 

The Security Council, 

Having examined the application of the Comoros (51) for admission to the United Nations, 

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Comoros be admitted to membership in the Unith Nations.


Footnotes

(51) Ibid., Supplement for October, November and December 1975, document S/ 11848.
