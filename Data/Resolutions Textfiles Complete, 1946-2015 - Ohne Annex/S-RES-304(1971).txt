Resolution 304 (1971) of 8 December 1971 

The Security Council, 

Having examined the application of the United Arab Emirates (67) for admission to the United Nations, 

Recommends to the General Assembly that the United Arab Emirates be admitted to membership in the United Nations.


Footnotes 

(67) Ibid., Supplement for October, November and December 1971, document S/ 10420.
