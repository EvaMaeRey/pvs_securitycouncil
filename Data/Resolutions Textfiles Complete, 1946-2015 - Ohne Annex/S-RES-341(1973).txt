The Security Council  
1.         Approves the report of the Secretary-General on the implementation of Security Council resolution 340 (1973) contained in document S/11052/Rev.1[1] dated 27 October 1973;  
2.         Decides that the Force shall be established in accordance with the above-mentioned report for an initial period of six months, and that it shall continue in operation thereafter, if required, provided the Security Council so decides.  
Adopted at the 1752nd meeting by 14 votes to none[2]            
[1] Ibid.      
[2] One member (China) did not participate in the voting.
