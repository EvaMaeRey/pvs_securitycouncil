The Security Council,  
Having considered the report of the Secretary-General on the United Nations Disengagement Observer Force (S/15079),  
Decides: (a) To call upon the parties concerned to implement immediately Security Council resolution 338 (1973) of 22 October 1973; (b) To renew the mandate of the United Nations Disengagement Observer Force to another period of six months, that is, until 30 November 1982; (c) To request the Secretary-General to submit, at the end of of this period, a report on the developments in the situation and the measures taken to implement Security Council resolution 338 (1973).
