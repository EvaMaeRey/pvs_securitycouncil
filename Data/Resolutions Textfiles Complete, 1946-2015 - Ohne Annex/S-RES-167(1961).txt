167 (1961). Resolution of 25 October 1961 
[S/4969] 

The Security Council, Having considered the application of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, (15)

Recommends to the General Assembly that the Islamic Republic of Mauritania be admitted to membership in the United Nations.
